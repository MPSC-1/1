Reservation Policy

Reservation policy for various categories as per Government of Maharashtra rules.

Category-wise Reservation Percentage

Category Reservation % Applicable For Documents Required
Scheduled Castes (SC) 13% All Posts Caste Certificate, Validity Certificate
Scheduled Tribes (ST) 7% All Posts Caste Certificate, Validity Certificate
Vimukta Jati (VJ) - NT-A 3% All Posts Caste Certificate, Validity Certificate
Nomadic Tribes (NT-B) 2.5% All Posts Caste Certificate, Validity Certificate
Nomadic Tribes (NT-C) 3.5% All Posts Caste Certificate, Validity Certificate
Nomadic Tribes (NT-D) 2% All Posts Caste Certificate, Validity Certificate
Other Backward Classes (OBC) 19% All Posts Caste Certificate, Non-Creamy Layer Certificate
Economically Weaker Sections (EWS) 10% All Posts Income Certificate, EWS Certificate
Persons with Disabilities (PwD) 4% (Horizontal) Identified Posts Disability Certificate

Important Notes

  • Reservation benefits are applicable only to candidates who are domiciled in Maharashtra State
  • Candidates must submit valid caste certificates issued by competent authorities
  • OBC candidates must submit Non-Creamy Layer Certificate valid for current financial year
  • EWS candidates must submit Income and Asset Certificate issued after 1st April of current financial year
  • PwD reservation is applicable only for posts identified as suitable for persons with disabilities
  • Women candidates get 30% reservation in posts where women candidates are eligible
